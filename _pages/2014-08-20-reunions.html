Professor Arnold E. Ross founded his program for high school students in 1957. Professor Ross's program continues to run for six weeks every summer.

Three Reunion-Conferences have celebrated the successes of this excellent program. Here are links to pages with further details about those events.

1996 Reunion-Conference (Ninetieth Birthday).

2001 Reunion-Conference (Ninety-fifth Birthday).

2007 Reunion-Conference (Fiftieth Anniversary).
