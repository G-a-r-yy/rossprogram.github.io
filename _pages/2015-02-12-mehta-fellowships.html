Two fellowships sponsored by Tara and Jasubhai Mehta will cover the cost of Ross Mathematics Program tuition, room and board, and travel expenses for exceptional Indian applicants who demonstrate financial need.

Eligibility Requirements

  1. Indian citizenship
  2. Age between 15 and 18 years
  3. Currently a full-time student at a high school in India
  4. Financial need (unable to cover program cost without assistance)
  5. Completion of the program application process as outlined below

Application Submission Instructions

Please submit all of the required Ross Program application documents located at u.osu.edu/rossmath/to-apply.Â  In addition, as part of your Mehta Fellowship application, please also submit a statement explaining your financial need to ross@math.osu.edu.
