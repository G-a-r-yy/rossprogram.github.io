Arnold Ross talking with students, during 1957-1958 academic year in Nieuwland Science Building, Notre Dame University. This newspaper photo was part of a story about Dr. Ross's "Sunday School" for talented high school students. Frank-Joseph Papp is in the back row on the far left. Next to him is Roger Peters.
