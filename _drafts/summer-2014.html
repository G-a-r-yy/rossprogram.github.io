Demographics: The 40 first-year students come from many places, with many of them highly trained in excellent high school programs. Consequences of the cutback from 8 weeks to 6.
One related point is with OSU’s new schedule the Spring semester ends before May 1 and Autumn classes start in mid-August. This forces us to be out of the dorms before August so that we have to start in mid-June. For instance, Ross students will arrive on Campus next summer on Sunday, June 14. Unfortunately, this early start date makes it impossible for students in some school districts to participate. This includes many New York students because required Regents Exams are typically given in late June.
