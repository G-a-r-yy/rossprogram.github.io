Glen Whitney was a principal mover in the founding of the Museum of Mathematics (MoMath). Planning began in 2008 and doors opened in New York (11 E 26th Street) in 2012. See http://momath.org/ for more information, along with the interesting lists of exhibits, events, and materials.
